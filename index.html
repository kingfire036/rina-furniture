About RINA FURNITURE

20+ Years of Crafting Excellence

Founded by Chandubhai Fataniya (Mistry), RINA FURNITURE has been transforming residential and commercial spaces across Ahmedabad for over two decades. Our passion lies in creating functional, beautiful, and durable interiors tailored to each client's unique vision.

We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with you from the initial concept to the final installation. Our expertise spans custom furniture, modular kitchens, innovative ceiling designs, precision painting, and complete turnkey solutions.

At RINA FURNITURE, quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and client satisfaction are the cornerstones of our business. Let us help you design your dream space.
